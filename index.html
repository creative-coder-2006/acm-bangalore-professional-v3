Our Mission

Fostering the Local Computing Community

The ACM Bangalore Professional Chapter is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive community of computing professionals by providing opportunities for networking, knowledge sharing, and dialogue across academia, industry, and research.

We bridge the gap between academia and industry — supporting professional growth through specialized seminars, technical workshops, and career advancement resources.
