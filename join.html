Membership Benefits

Everything you gain as a member of the ACM Bangalore Professional Chapter.

Professional Networking

Connect with 500+ computing professionals, researchers, and academics across Bangalore's vibrant tech ecosystem.

Eminence & Showcase

Opportunities to present your work, share expertise, and build your professional reputation within the local and global ACM community.

Mentoring Opportunities

Access formal and informal mentoring relationships with senior professionals and researchers across academia and industry.

Knowledge Sharing

Be part of a knowledge-sharing community — attend exclusive seminars, workshops, and fireside chats not available to the general public.

Local Technical Content

Access high-quality, locally curated technical content from India's leading computing professionals — relevant to the Bangalore and India tech ecosystem.
