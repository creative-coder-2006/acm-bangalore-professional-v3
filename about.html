Our Story

About ACM Bangalore

A legacy of innovation, excellence, and community — advancing computing as a science and profession in India's technology hub.

Mission Statement

Advancing Computing in Bangalore

To foster a vibrant, inclusive local community of computing professionals by providing opportunities for networking, knowledge sharing, and dialogue across academia, industry, and research.

We support the professional growth of our members through specialized seminars, technical workshops, and career advancement resources in the ever-evolving field of computing.

The ACM Bangalore Professional Chapter was established to serve as a focal point for computer scientists, IT professionals, and researchers in Bangalore. Today, we stand as one of the most active professional chapters in the region, hosting world-class conferences, technical talks, and workshops that empower our members to stay at the forefront of the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Our Programs

What We Do

A range of programs and activities designed to advance the computing profession in Bangalore.

Seminars & Technical Workshops

Specialized seminars and hands-on workshops covering cutting-edge topics in AI, systems design, quantum computing, cloud architecture, and more — led by industry veterans and academic leaders.

Professional Networking

Regular networking events, fireside chats, and panel discussions that connect computing professionals across academia, industry, and research organizations in Bangalore and beyond.

Career Advancement Resources

Access to ACM's world-class digital library, learning resources, mentoring opportunities, and career development programs to help members stay ahead in the rapidly evolving computing landscape.

Knowledge Sharing & Research Dialogue

Creating platforms for meaningful dialogue between academia and industry — including publication opportunities, research showcases, and collaborative initiatives that drive innovation in computing.
